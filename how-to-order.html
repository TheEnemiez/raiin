How to Place an Order

Ordering from RAIIN Services is quick and easy! Follow these simple steps:

  1. Contact Us

    To begin, join our Discord server and navigate to the relevant channel for the service you wish to order. Use the /buy command to initiate your order.

    • Sky Overlays: Sky Overlays [Discord]
    • Cloak Designs: Cloak Designs [Discord]
    • Thumbnail Designs: Thumbnail Designs [Discord]
  2. Specify Your Request

    Once in the appropriate channel, clearly outline your request using the /buy command. Be sure to include details such as desired themes, colors, and any specific elements you want in your design.

  3. Confirmation and Payment

    After placing your order, we will send you a confirmation message along with a payment link or details, depending on your selected method. Please ensure the payment is completed to start the production of your customized order.

  4. Expected Delivery

    Your custom order will typically be completed within 3-5 business days, depending on the complexity. We will keep you updated on the progress and notify you when your order is ready for download.

Frequently Asked Questions

    Can I request revisions?

    Yes, minor revisions are allowed within the initial requirements.

    What if I need a rush delivery?

    Please mention this in your initial request. A rush fee may apply.

    Is there a refund policy?

    Refunds are not typically provided unless there is a failure to deliver the promised product. Each case is reviewed individually.

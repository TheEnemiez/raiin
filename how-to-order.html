How to Place an Order

Ordering from RAIIN Services is quick and easy! Follow these simple steps:

  1. Contact Us

    To begin, join our Discord server and go to any channel.

    • Sky Overlays: Sky Overlays [Discord]
    • Cloak Designs: Cloak Designs [Discord]
  2. Specify Your Request

    Once there, use the /buy command to start your order. Once you've done that, follow the instructions provided by the discord bot.

  3. Confirmation and Payment

    After placing your order, the bot will send you a confirmation message along with a payment link. Please ensure the payment is completed to start the production of your customized order.

  4. Expected Delivery

    Your custom order will typically be completed within 1-2 business days, depending on the complexity. You will be updated throughout the process and notified you when your order is ready for download.

Frequently Asked Questions

    Can I request revisions?

    Yes, minor revisions are allowed within the initial requirements.

    What if I need a rush delivery?

    So far there's no priority for who receives their order first. May change in the future.

    Is there a refund policy?

    Refunds are not typically provided unless there is a failure to deliver the promised product. Each case is reviewed individually.
